People

A diverse team of creative designers and developers headed up by a very experienced group of directors.

Mark Luckett

Managing Director
Founder, with over 30 years experience in both the 'business to business' & 'business to employee' sectors, he is passionate about helping clients deliver innovative design solutions to their complex marketing & communications challenges.
A key strength is his ability to blend design & technology to deliver great user centric solutions & experiences.
Mark Bowerman

Director
Mark joined Arthaus in its infancy and has been a big part of the company’s growth & development. Always keen to engage with the client to understand their requirements and those of their target audience. Mark is a project leader for digital media projects as well as heading up new business generation.
Becky Carter

Creative Director
Becky began her career as a designer in Fleet Street & after seven successful years working in London she joined Arthaus where now she enjoys her role as Creative Director. With her wealth of experience & hands on approach, Becky oversees our talented creative team in all aspects of branding, design & communications.
